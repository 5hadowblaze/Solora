# Solora Product Design

**Status:** Superseded by simplified v1
**Tagline:** Your life becomes your lore.

## Simplified v1

Solora is a private career-memory journal: capture a reflection, revisit it in Lore, and keep a Master CV beside the evidence that supports it. Share offers only two lightweight outputs: a post from recent moments and a personal CV summary as memory bubbles.

Calendar connection, deck generation, and the career-graph / generated-world-manifest direction are intentionally out of scope. The earlier hackathon specification is retained as [historical product exploration](superpowers/specs/2026-07-18-solora-product-design.md), not the current build target.
